The Beagle Blog

The Beagle is a breed of small hound,similar in apperance to the much
larger foxhound.The Beagle was developed primarily for huntinghare, known as
beagling.Possesing a great sense of smell and superior tracking instincts,the
Beagle is the primary breed used as a detectiondog for prohibited agricultural
imports and foodstuffs in quarantine around the world.The Beagle is intelligent
ans is a popular pet due to its size,good temper,and a lack of inherited health
problems.
